Mango Curry Rice

Experience the perfect blend of sweet and spicy with this delectable mango curry rice recipe. Tender chunks of mango, aromatic spices,
and creamy coconut milk create a symphony of flavors, perfectly complemented by fluffy basmati rice. Indulge in this tropical twist on
a classic curry dish that is sure to transport your taste buds to paradise.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 lb boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet.
    Cook for another minute, stirring occasionally.
  3. Incorporate the curry powder, turmeric powder,
    cumin powder, and coriander powder. Toast the spices
    for about a minute to unlock their flavors.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until
    browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and stir well to combine.
    Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  6. Add the bell pepper slices and cubed mangoes to the skillet.
    Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for about
    15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors
    are well blended.
  7. Serve the sizzling spiced mango curry over steamed jasmine rice,
    garnishing with fresh cilantro leaves
